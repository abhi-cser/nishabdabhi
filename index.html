About

जिन्दगी की स्याही से कलम की धार तक

Abhishek Kumar, known artistically as Nishabd Abhi, weaves the silence left behind by unsaid words. While engineering grounds him in logic by day, it is through the vulnerability of verse—shayari, poetry, and ghazals—that he truly navigates the world.

Nishabd (निशब्द) means silent or speechless—the profound quiet before a storm of emotions. His pen bleeds the colors of life, capturing the delicate nuances of love, longing, and the profound beauty of ordinary moments. Every couplet is a mirror held up to the soul.

He is also a core voice behind The Backloggers, a sprawling literary collective and sanctuary for budding talents across poetry, art, and music. His debut single, Kaise Bataau, was born from this exact tension—how to express an ocean of feeling when you have only a few drops of words.
